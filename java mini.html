The Vidyavardhini's College of Engineering and Technology(VCET), is an engineering college in Vasai-Virar. The college is affiliated to University of Mumbai and offers Bachelor's degree in Engineering. The college has been graded 'A' by the DTE. The five branches Computer Engineering, Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering, Information Technology Engineering, Instrumentation Engineering, and Mechanical Engineering have been accredited by National Board of Accreditation(NBA) for the period of 3 years w.e.f.
